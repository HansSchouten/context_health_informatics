Convert is used to automatically generate behavior of web site response.

Syntax

  • CONVERT PHASE
  • CONVERT COL(column)
  • CONVERT REMEASUREMENT COL(column)
  • CONVERT SECOND MEASUREMENT COL(column)

Examples

  • CONVERT COL(Creatinine) PER 7 DAYS
    Converts the data of the measured Creatinine column into feedback based on the kreatinine status of the last two days.
  • CONVERT REMEASUREMENT COL(Creatinine) PER 7 DAYS
    Converts the data of the measured Creatinine column and checks whether the patient has to repeat the measurements on the next day.
