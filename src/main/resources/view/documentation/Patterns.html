Find a section of records starting with the record containing the first and ending with the record containing the last label of the defined pattern. WITHIN defines a maximum duration and AFTER defines a minium duration between two labels.

Syntax

  • LABEL(A) WITHIN(1) LABEL(B)
    Find occurences of label A and on the same day label B
  • LABEL(A) WITHIN(2) LABEL(B)
    Find occurences of label A and on the same day, or on the next day label B
  • LABEL(A) AFTER(1) LABEL(B)
    Find occurences of label A and on the next day or later label B
  • LABEL(A) AFTER(2) LABEL(B)
    Find occurences of label A and later than the next day label B
  • LABEL(A) AFTER(2) WITHIN(3) LABEL(B)
    Find occurences of label A and 2 days later label B

Examples

  • LABEL WITH measured_right WHERE COL(phase) = 1 AFTER PATTERN LABEL(entered) AFTER(1) WITHIN(2) LABEL(entered)
    Labels every record that is entered one day after the last entered record with 'measured_right', but only if the pattern ends with a record that satisfies the condition: phase equals 1.
